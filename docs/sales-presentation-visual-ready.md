## Presentation Guidance
### Keep On-Slide Text Short
This version is designed for presentation visuals, not dense reading.

### Let the Speaker Expand Verbally
The presenter should explain examples, context, and use cases live.

### Emphasise These Themes
- operational clarity
- hospitality relevance
- practical technology
- business value

### Avoid
- overcrowded slides
- long paragraphs
- generic AI hype
- overcomplicated diagrams too early
