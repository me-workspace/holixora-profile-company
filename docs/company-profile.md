# Holixora Company Profile

## Company Overview
Holixora Digital Technology is an AI automation and software solutions company focused on helping hospitality and related service businesses improve the way they operate.

We design practical systems that reduce friction, improve coordination, and strengthen day-to-day operations through automation, workflow design, and tailored software.

## Vision
To help hospitality and related service businesses move toward smarter, more efficient operations through practical automation and purpose-built software systems.

## Mission
To build digital solutions that reduce manual work, improve operational visibility, and support business growth with greater clarity and control.

## What We Do
Holixora provides AI automation and software solutions designed around real business operations.

### Service Areas
- AI automation for repetitive and process-heavy workflows
- Workflow analysis and automation roadmap planning
- Custom business software development
- Internal dashboards and operational systems
- Hospitality-focused digital solutions
- Prototype and MVP development
