# Holixora Proposal Template

## Purpose
A reusable proposal structure for Holixora to present recommended solutions clearly after discovery or consultation.

## Proposal Style
- clear
- practical
- premium
- business-focused
- solution-oriented

The proposal should feel like strategic guidance, not just a freelance quotation.

---

# Proposal Structure

## 1. Cover Page
### Title
**Proposal**

### Subtitle
Prepared by **Holixora Digital Technology**

### Fields
- Client name
- Company name
- Proposal title
- Date
- Prepared by

---

## 2. Executive Summary
### Purpose
A short summary of the business need, the proposed solution, and the intended result.

### Template
Thank you for the opportunity to understand your business needs.

Based on our discussion, the key challenge appears to be **[main problem]**, which is currently affecting **[area of operations / efficiency / visibility / coordination]**.

Holixora recommends **[recommended solution]** to help improve **[desired outcome]**.

The goal of this proposal is to outline a practical path toward stronger systems, smoother workflows, and clearer operational execution.

---

## 3. Current Situation / Business Context
### Purpose
Show that Holixora understands the client’s business environment.

### Template
From our discussion, the current business environment includes:
- [current workflow situation]
- [current system limitation]
- [manual process issue]
- [reporting / visibility issue]

These factors are creating friction in **[specific business area]** and limiting the business’s ability to operate with greater efficiency and control.

---

## 4. Key Challenges Identified
### Purpose
Clarify the business pain points the proposal addresses.

### Template
The main challenges identified are:
- [challenge 1]
- [challenge 2]
- [challenge 3]
- [challenge 4]

### Supporting Line
If left unresolved, these challenges may continue to slow operations, increase manual effort, and reduce visibility across the business.

---

## 5. Recommended Solution
### Purpose
Present the proposed path clearly.

### Template
Holixora recommends the following solution:

**[solution name]**

This solution is designed to:
- reduce [manual work / delays / duplication]
- improve [visibility / coordination / reporting]
- support [better workflows / better control / stronger operations]
- create a stronger foundation for [growth / efficiency / system clarity]

### Example solution names
- AI Automation Audit
- Workflow Automation Implementation
- Custom Software Development
- Hospitality Operations Solution
- Discovery & Planning Engagement

---

## 6. Scope of Work
### Purpose
Define what is included.

### Template
The proposed scope of work includes:
- [scope item 1]
- [scope item 2]
- [scope item 3]
- [scope item 4]
- [scope item 5]

### Optional note
Any items not listed above can be discussed separately as future phases or additional scope.

---

## 7. Deliverables
### Purpose
Make the output concrete.

### Template
Deliverables may include:
- workflow assessment / audit report
- process recommendations
- automation logic design
- dashboard or system planning
- software prototype
- implementation roadmap
- completed workflow automation setup
- documentation / handover notes

---

## 8. Project Approach
### Purpose
Show how Holixora works.

### Template
Holixora’s approach is designed to keep the work practical and aligned with business needs.

### Phases
**1. Discover**  
Understand workflows, pain points, and current system gaps.

**2. Define**  
Clarify the right solution and scope.

**3. Design & Build**  
Develop the recommended automation, system, or software solution.

**4. Refine**  
Review usability, alignment, and implementation quality.

---

## 9. Timeline
### Purpose
Set expectation on delivery timing.

### Template
Estimated timeline:
- Phase 1: [time period]
- Phase 2: [time period]
- Phase 3: [time period]
- Total estimated duration: [X weeks]

### Note
Final timeline may vary depending on project complexity, feedback cycles, and approval speed.

---

## 10. Commercial Structure
### Purpose
Present pricing cleanly.

### Template
### Option A — Fixed Project Fee
- Scope: [summary]
- Investment: [amount]

### Option B — Phase-Based Fee
- Phase 1: [amount]
- Phase 2: [amount]
- Phase 3: [amount]

### Option C — Discovery First
- Initial audit / discovery fee: [amount]
- Further implementation scoped after discovery

### Note
Pricing should reflect business value and project scope, not just hours.

---

## 11. Assumptions / Dependencies
### Purpose
Protect clarity and reduce misunderstandings.

### Template
This proposal assumes:
- timely client feedback
- access to relevant workflow or system information
- clarity on decision-maker approvals
- scope alignment before implementation begins

Additional integrations, extended revisions, or major scope changes may affect timeline and pricing.

---

## 12. Why Holixora
### Purpose
Reinforce why Holixora is the right fit.

### Template
Holixora combines automation, workflow thinking, and software capability to help hospitality and related service businesses improve the way they operate.

Our approach is:
- operational-first
- practical, not overcomplicated
- tailored to business reality
- focused on long-term system value

---

## 13. Next Step
### Purpose
Make it easy to move forward.

### Template
If this proposal aligns with your business goals, the next step would be:
- approve the proposed direction
- confirm scope and commercial structure
- schedule project kickoff / discovery session

---

## 14. Closing
### Template
Thank you for the opportunity to present this proposal.

Holixora would be pleased to support your business in building stronger systems, smoother workflows, and better operational clarity.

---

## 15. Optional Proposal Variants
### Variant A — Audit Proposal
Use when selling a low-friction entry engagement.

### Variant B — Automation Proposal
Use when a workflow pain point is already clear.

### Variant C — Software Build Proposal
Use when a custom system or dashboard is needed.

### Variant D — Hospitality Operations Proposal
Use for more strategic or integrated hospitality projects.

---

## 16. Proposal Writing Rules
### Do
- keep it clear and structured
- show understanding of business pain
- focus on outcomes
- make next steps easy

### Don’t
- overload with technical jargon
- make it feel like a generic dev quote
- leave scope vague
- hide dependencies or assumptions
