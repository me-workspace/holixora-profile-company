# Holixora Outreach Templates

## Purpose
Turn Holixora’s positioning and sales strategy into usable messages for outreach, follow-up, and consultation conversations.

## Messaging Principle
Lead with business pain and business value.

Do not lead with too much technical detail.
Do not sound like a generic marketing agency.
Do not sound like a random freelancer offering everything.

Holixora should sound like:
- practical
- credible
- operationally focused
- hospitality-relevant
- business-minded

---

## 12. Discovery Questions
Use these during early conversations.

### Operational questions
- Which workflows currently take more time than they should?
- Where does information get lost or delayed?
- What is still being handled manually today?
- Which parts of the business feel hardest to coordinate?

### Systems questions
- What tools are you using now?
- Are there disconnected systems or duplicated work?
- Where do you lack visibility across operations?

### Business questions
- What is slowing growth or making operations harder?
- What would improvement look like for you?
- Is the priority more automation, better reporting, or a stronger internal system?

---

## 13. Messaging Rules
### Do
- keep messages short
- focus on business pain and outcomes
- sound practical and credible
- make the next step easy

### Don’t
- over-explain the technology
- sound too broad
- use too many AI buzzwords
- make the first message too long

---

## 14. Recommended Sequence
### First touch
Use warm intro or cold outreach depending on lead source.

### Second touch
Use follow-up message #1.

### Third touch
Use follow-up message #2.

### Once interest exists
Move into consultation invite + discovery questions.
